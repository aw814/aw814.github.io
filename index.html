Research Summary

I develop human-centered AI systems that promote epistemic equity and cultural inclusion. My work bridges multilingual natural language processing and emotionally expressive, adaptive AI, with the goal of making AI not only accurate and transparent, but also a medium for empathy, cultural awareness, and human well-being.

Research

Research Interests

I study human-centered, socio-technical AI systems that support epistemic equity and human well-being. My work focuses on:

Multilingual AI

Investigating how multilingual models can surface underrepresented knowledge across languages, addressing the limitations of current systems that privilege dominant perspectives.

Expressive & Adaptive AI

Designing emotionally expressive, adaptive AI systems that foster transparency and empathy, reframing AI not only as a computational tool but also as a medium for cultural inclusion and human connection.
